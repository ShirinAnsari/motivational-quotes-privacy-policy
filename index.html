Privacy Policy for Inspire - Motivational Quotes

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Thank you for using Inspire - Motivational Quotes ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our motivational quotes application on Android.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Do Not Provide

Inspire - Motivational Quotes does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Automatically Collected Information

Usage Data via Firebase

The App uses Google Firebase Realtime Database to sync quote content. Firebase may automatically collect certain technical data, including device identifiers, IP address, and app usage diagnostics. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising Data via Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), IP address, and interaction data with ads to serve relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings. AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your saved/favorite quotes (stored in a local Room/SQLite database)
  • App preferences such as dark mode settings and notification time (stored in DataStore)

This local data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Deliver and display motivational quotes content
  • Serve advertisements within the App via Google AdMob
  • Send daily motivational quote notifications (only if you enable this feature)
  • Improve app stability and performance via Firebase diagnostics

3. Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the App will send a daily motivational quote notification at a time you configure in Settings. You can disable this feature at any time from within the App's Settings screen or from your device's system notification settings.

The App also requests the RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED permission solely to reschedule your daily notification after a device restart. No other data is collected through this permission.

4. Internet and Network Permissions

The App requests the following network-related permissions:

  • INTERNET — to sync quote content from Firebase Realtime Database and load advertisements.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — to check network connectivity and optimize data syncing.

These permissions are used only for the purposes described above.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Realtime Database Quote content sync Google Privacy Policy
Google AdMob In-app advertising Google Privacy Policy
Android WorkManager Background notification scheduling Part of Android OS

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any other third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. Data retained locally on your device (favorites, preferences) persists until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data from your device settings. Data collected by Firebase and AdMob is retained per Google's data retention policies.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access and correction: You may review and modify your in-app preferences at any time in the Settings screen.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Delete local data: Uninstall the App or clear app data via Settings → Apps → Inspire → Clear Data.
  • Disable notifications: Turn off via Settings → Apps → Inspire → Notifications or within the App's Settings screen.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect data. All quote data synced via Firebase is transmitted over HTTPS. Local data is stored in the Android app's private storage and is not accessible to other apps.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: zsdevelopers4@gmail.com
